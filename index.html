Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open your email client.
  2. Compose a new email.
  3. In the "To" field, enter the following email address: 8quadtech@gmail.com
  4. In the subject line, write "Account Deletion Request".
  5. In the body of the email, include your account details and the reason for the deletion request.
  6. Send the email.

We will process your request and notify you once your account has been deleted.
